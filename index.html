Marine Biodiversity

The ocean is Earth's largest ecosystem, home to over 230,000 known species. From tiny plankton to massive blue whales, every creature plays a vital role in the marine food chain.

Ocean Pollution Threat

Plastic pollution, oil spills, and industrial waste are severely threatening marine ecosystems. By 2050, there could be more plastic than fish in our oceans.

Climate Change Impact

Oceans absorb about 30% of human-produced CO2, causing acidification. Global warming also leads to rising sea levels and coral bleaching.

Overfishing Crisis
